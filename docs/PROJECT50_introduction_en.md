## Core Philosophy of Project 50

The essence of the Project 50 challenge lies in self-discipline and habit formation. It encourages participants to stick to a carefully designed set of core rules over 50 days, gradually building a more positive and healthier lifestyle. These rules cover several key areas, aiming to help you:

1. Regular Schedule, Efficient Living: Start with early rising, control your daily rhythm, say goodbye to procrastination, and improve efficiency.
2. Focused Growth, Self-Improvement: Utilize the quiet morning hours for personal growth and knowledge accumulation.
3. Physical Strength, Vibrant Energy: Build strength, boost energy, and achieve a healthier body through daily exercise.
4. Continuous Learning, Expanding Horizons: Continuously expand your knowledge boundaries and enhance personal competitiveness through reading and learning new skills.
5. Healthy Diet, Nourishing Body and Mind: Focus on healthy eating, provide quality energy for your body, and improve your physical and mental state.
6. Deep Reflection, Continuous Progress: Daily reflection and summary, review progress, identify areas for improvement, and continuously optimize and enhance.

## Seven Challenge Rules

- **RULE 1: WAKE UP BEFORE 8 A.M.**: But sleep 6+ hours and try to maintain a consistent bed routine with enough sleep/ make this rule fit to your individual needs.
- **RULE 2: DO YOUR MORNING ROUTINE**: Do your morning routine: 1h no distractions. (no phone and social media. If enough time, also meditate, go for a walk or simply deep-think and mentally prepare for the day.)
- **RULE 3: EXERCISE FOR 1H A DAY**: Exercise based on your fitness level. (Includes walking, stretching, running, yoga, bike gym etc...)
- **RULE 4: READ 10 PAGES A DAY**: Read motivational, psychological OR business-related books (also educational books count.)
- **RULE 5: DEDICATE 1H TOWARDS A NEW SKILL**: Prepare goals and anticipated skills before the challenge starts (prep phase). No distractions and high concentration while doing.
- **RULE 6: FOLLOW A HEALTHY DIET**: Follow a diet of your choice. (no alcohol, no soft drinks and generally less sugar for example). Also drink enough water.
- **RULE 7: TRACK YOUR DAILY PROGRESS IN A JOURNAL**: Write down positive and negative experiences, what you've learned and where you can improve.

### GENERAL PROJECT50 RULE

If you fail on any of the 50 days, start over at day 1. Repeat until finished.
